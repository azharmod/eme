EME Resources Sdn Bhd is a mechanical and electrical engineering services company founded
in 2013 in Johor Bahru. Recognising the potential and needs of efficient mechanical and electrical engineering services provider in Malaysia, the founders have been steering the company steadily forward from its inception, first in the efficient energy management services, steam engineering, mechanical pipings, electrical, process control then progressing to fire fighting and protection systems. With growth, the company has added even more services, from boiler servicing, utility pipeworks, to fire fighting system, construction and more, to offer clients a wide range of engineering services, maintenance, construction, fabrication, consultancy and various value added services according to industries need.

With a core staff of engineers, EME Resources is well positioned to offer expertise and knowledge in mechanical and electrical system design, supply, installation and maintenance especially steam engineering and fire protection services, to consumers and businesses. Staff are regularly kept upto- date on the latest technology and hands-on training is an absolute must.

Armed with these strengths, EME Resources is well poised to provide Malaysia as well as the
international market the technology and services required by various sectors and industries.
